> A living list of learning resources I've found genuinely useful. Not ranked, grows over time.

## AI & LLMs

- [Anthropic docs](https://docs.anthropic.com/) — Claude API, prompt engineering guides
- [fast.ai Practical Deep Learning](https://course.fast.ai/) — hands-on deep learning, free
- [Neural Networks and Deep Learning](http://neuralnetworksanddeeplearning.com/) — free online book by Michael Nielsen
- [The Deep Learning textbook](https://www.deeplearningbook.org/) — Goodfellow et al., free online

## Embedded & Hardware

- [AVR Libc Reference Manual](https://www.nongnu.org/avr-libc/user-manual/) — authoritative AVR C library docs
- [Exploring Raspberry Pi (book)](https://www.exploringrpi.com/) — Derek Molloy's hands-on guide

## Software Engineering

- [Python Data Science Handbook](https://jakevdk.github.io/PythonDataScienceHandbook/) — Jake VanderPlas, free online
- [Learn You Some Erlang](https://learnyousomeerlang.com/content) — Erlang fundamentals, free online
- [Elixir Getting Started](https://elixir-lang.org/getting-started/introduction.html) — official Elixir tutorial
- [Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/) — datasets, competitions, free courses

## Math & Theory

- [Neural Network Design (pdf)](https://hagan.okstate.edu/NNDesign.pdf) — Hagan et al., free textbook
